Simple words when you sick in Korean

  • If you can't say these then you can show these to Doctor.

Ophtalmology = 안과


[In Ophthalmology]


-My eyes feel itchy.

-My eyes feel irritate.

-I have dim eyes.

-I am seeing double.

-I need a new pair of glasses.

-I get a headache when I wear my glasses.

-I guess my eyesight is getting worse.

-I lost one of my contact lenses.

-I am near-sighted.

-I am far-sighted.
