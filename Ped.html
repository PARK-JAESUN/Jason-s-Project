Simple words when you sick in Korean

  • If you can't say these then you can show these to Doctor.

Pediatrics = 소아과


[In Pediatrics]


-My baby doesn't nurse very well.

-He/She wheezes when he/she breathes.

-His/Her nose is stuffed.

-He/She looks pale.

-He/She coughs severely.

-He/She doesn't have much appetite.

-He/She has a runny nose.

-He/She vomits his milk.

-He/She has a splinter in his finger.
