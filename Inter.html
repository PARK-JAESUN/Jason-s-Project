Simple words when you sick in Korean

  • If you can't say these then you can show these to Doctor.

Internal Medicine = 내과


[In Internal Medicine]


1.Headache

-I have a headache.

-I have a splitting headache.

-My head feels heavy.

-I feel dizzy = My head is spinning.


2.Cold

-I guess I've got a cold.

-I sneeze repeatedly.

-I have a runny nose. or My nose keeps running.

-I hurt all over. = I ache all over.

-I have a fever.

-I fell chill.

-I have a pain in my chest. = My chest hurts.

-My back is sore. = I have a sore back.

-I have a stiff neck/shoulder.

-I have a pain in my right/left side.

-I have a sore throat. And, I can't swallow.


3.Digestion

-I have little appetite and cannot sleep well.

-I throw up everything I eat.

-I have gas in my stomach.

-I have a stomachache.

-I belch a lot.

-I feel like vomiting.

-I am nauseated.


4.Etc

-I have a bee sting.

-My nose is bleeding.

-I am not sure what's wrong.
